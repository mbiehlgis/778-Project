War Crimes in The Pacific:
Horrors in the Far East

Created By:
Michael Biehl

One of the greatest failures in education is the outright neglection of history, especially histroy that continues to heavily impact the world today. A quintessential case of this can be found in how the American education system's learning material on World War II is disproportionately Euro-centric with stark ignorance of how the war raged on in the other half of the world. The periods before and between Pearl Harbor and the atomic bombing of Hiroshima goes entirely untouched. The innate issue with this is that American students are not learning the events and the causes behind them that lead to horrific scenes in the far east, which could in turn pave a path for the world to repeat those mistakes. The intent behind this interactive map is to start a conversation about these events after the free exploration of the interactive.
